Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2024

Welcome to our video player app! We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your data. We solemnly promise: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

Core Principle: Your Data Belongs to You

Our core design philosophy is to protect user privacy to the greatest extent possible. All data processing, whenever possible, is done locally on your device. We do not maintain servers to store user data, nor do we share your information with any third parties.

We Do Not Collect Any Information

This app does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or any other form of user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.
  • Your geographic location information.
  • Your device unique identifiers (such as UDID, IDFA).
  • Any analytics data, advertising identifiers, or user behavior tracking information.

On-Device Data Processing

Some advanced features of this app require data processing, but all such processing is done offline on your device:

  • Files and Playback History: Video files you open through this app, playback history, playback progress, and video metadata (such as filename, duration) are stored only in your device's local storage. This data is completely under your control and can be deleted at any time within the app. We cannot access this information.
  • Speech Recognition (Subtitle Generation): To generate subtitles from video audio tracks, this app will request your "Speech Recognition" permission. We use Apple's speech recognition framework and enforce offline recognition on your device. Your audio data never leaves your device and is not sent to Apple or us.
  • Text Translation: When you translate generated subtitles, we use Apple's translation framework. This feature prioritizes offline translation on your device. For some language pairs requiring online support, data will be sent directly by your operating system to Apple's servers for processing, following Apple's privacy policy. This app does not intercept, store, or forward this information.

Permission Requests Explained

To provide core functionality, this app may request the following permissions:

  • File Access Permission: You need to authorize this app to access video files or folders you wish to play through the system file picker. We use Apple's security mechanism (Security-Scoped Bookmarks) to maintain access to files after your authorization, avoiding the need to reselect each time. We never access files you have not explicitly authorized.
  • Speech Recognition Permission: This permission is requested when you need to use the "Speech to Subtitle" feature. If you decline, it will only affect this specific feature; other app functions will continue to work normally.

In-App Purchases

This app includes in-app purchases (subscription services) processed through the Apple App Store. All transaction processes are handled directly by Apple. We do not obtain any of your payment information (such as credit card numbers) or personal account information. We only store your subscription status locally on your device (for example, whether you are an active subscriber) to unlock premium features.

No Third-Party Integration

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media SDKs. Your usage behavior is completely anonymous.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or by posting updates on our App Store page. We recommend you review this policy periodically for the latest information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our App Store support page.
